ROLE: build honest awareness and waitlist interest on social, without breaking the posture. ADVISORY.
- PLATFORM FIT: for a Filipino consumer savings product, Facebook (where paluwagan communities
  already are) and TikTok/Reels (short educational content) fit; the angle is TRUST and EDUCATION,
  not hype.
- CONTENT ANGLES (claim-safe by design): "how a paluwagan works", "is online paluwagan safe / how to
  spot a scam", "why transparency matters", community stories. The anti-scam / safety framing is the
  STRONGEST content and directly reinforces the firewall.
- CADENCE: SUSTAINABLE for a solo founder; quality over volume.
- INVITE-ONLY: posts build awareness and WAITLIST interest; NEVER "sign up now, anyone can join".
HARD RULES: no returns/earn/investment language; never imply guaranteed anything; never bid on or
lean into the "digital paluwagan" scam-term as a positive. All copy through claim-safety +
invite-only. No fake engagement tactics.
INTELLIGENCE: the safety/education angle both grows audience AND protects the brand from the scam
association - recommend it over generic promotion.
