How paluwagan works

One group, one amount, everyone gets a turn.

A paluwagan is the savings circle Filipinos have run for generations. Paluwagan the app keeps the same simple idea, it just makes every step visible.

1
Form your circle

Gather your pamilya, katrabaho, or neighbors. Everyone agrees on one amount per round and how often to pay.

2
Everyone contributes

Each round, every member puts in the same amount. Each payment is logged and visible to the whole group.

3
Take your turn

Every round the whole pot goes to one member, until everyone has had a turn. You get back exactly what you put in.
